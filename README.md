# 🎓 Factors Affecting the Choices of Higher Educational Services  
### 📊 Power BI Research & Insights Dashboard

---

## 🎯 Project Overview
This project analyzes the **key factors influencing how students choose higher educational institutions**.  

Using structured **survey data**, the dashboard explores how **psychological factors, social influence, advertising, and information sources** shape student decision-making.

The analysis highlights patterns in **student needs, perceptions, and trusted advice channels**, helping identify what drives education choices and institutional preferences.

---

## 🔍 Analysis Objectives

- Identify the core **needs, motivations, and perceptions** of students when selecting higher education institutions  
- Measure the influence of **advertising, parental guidance, peer recommendations, and cultural similarity**  
- Analyze which **information sources students rely on most** during decision-making  
- Visualize **Likert-scale response patterns** across multiple influence factors  
- Present findings through a **two-page interactive Power BI dashboard**

---

## 🧹 Data Preparation

Data preparation was performed using **Excel**, including:

- Cleaning survey responses  
- Standardizing categorical fields  
- Structuring Likert-scale variables for analysis  
- Preparing fields for **Power BI modeling and visualization**

## 📊 Dashboard Structure

### 📄 Page 1 – Student Profile & Decision Drivers

Focuses on **student demographics and key decision drivers**.

**Key Components**

📊 **Interactive Slicers**
- Education level
- Demographic filters

📌 **KPI Cards**
- **Most Common Student Need**
- **Most Trusted Advice Source**

📈 **Visual Insights**
- Education level distribution  
- Preferred graduate outcomes  
- Awareness and advertising channels  
- Major influence factors affecting student decisions  

---

### 📄 Page 2 – Influence Factor Deep Dive

Explores **comparative strength of different decision drivers**.

**Visual Components**

 Likert-scale averages across influence categories  
 Composite indices for factor comparison  
 Ranked horizontal bar chart of influence strength  
 Python-powered **boxplots** showing response distribution  

These visuals help identify **which factors have the strongest impact on student choices**.

---

## ⚙️ Technical Highlights

- **Power BI dashboard development** for research insights  
- **DAX measures** for averages, ranking, and mode-based indicators  
- **Python visuals integration** for distribution analysis  
- **Excel-based data cleaning and preprocessing**

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **Social influence and trusted advice sources** play a major role in education decisions  
- Advertising channels contribute to awareness but are **less influential than personal recommendations**  
- Student needs and motivations vary across **education levels and demographics**  
- Boxplot distributions highlight **variation in sentiment across decision factors**

These insights can support **institutional marketing strategies and student outreach initiatives**.
